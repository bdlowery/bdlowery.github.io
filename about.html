About me

I live in Rocklin, CA. I went to Sierra College for 2 years and then transferred to Sacramento State. I'll be graduating from Sac state in Spring 2024.

I'm currently working at Perpetual Education. My current project is revamping their internal student dashboard to be more custom, and to give instructors more quality of life. The site is built with custom wordpress. The languages I'm using are PHP and Javascript.

I co-founded a web development agency called Unwrap Design. A lot of small businesses have crappy websites, and can't afford to spend $10k on a modern, nice looking website (and most often when they do spend a large sum, the site still looks bad).

We created an agency using a subscription model to try and solve this problem. Currently have 10 recurring subscriptions, and are working on 4 more sites.
